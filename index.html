Blend

Back and full of joy, Christmas Blend is here to make any celebration shine. Our historic recipe perfectly combines aged Sumatra beans with beans from Guatemala, Colombia and Papua New Guinea. The rich, robust flavor complements traditions new and old with sweet, velvety notes of spicy chocolate and fir tips. With each cup, make the holidays all yours.

Tostat

Lightly roasted beans from West Java, Papua New Guinea with washed and sun-dried coffees from East Africa. This combination creates the perfect mix of festivity, with soft woody aromas to match. Bright notes of orange spice and fig bring out the syrupy sweetness of maple in this cup that makes every moment a celebration.
